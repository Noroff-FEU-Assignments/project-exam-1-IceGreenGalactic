About us

Welcome to Kennel Shirkus, a small, love-filled breeding of black/white and silver-grey large poodles. The kennel is owned and run by Bente, a dedicated dog enthusiast with over 25 years of experience in poodle breeds.

Our History

Kennel Shirkus was founded as a result of a love for poodles and a commitment to breeding healthy, beautiful and well-adjusted dogs. Bente has been involved in the world of poodles for over a quarter of a century and has dedicated her life to improving the breed.

Our breeding

We specialize in black/white and silver-grey large poodles, and every puppy born in Kennel Shirkus is greeted with love and care from day one. We emphasize breeding healthy and well-balanced dogs with good temperaments and excellent breed standards. Our poodles live with us as beloved family members and we are proud to say that they have wonderful personalities and are highly socialized.

Our goal

Our primary goal is to breed poodles that are not only beautiful and healthy, but also bring joy and love to their future families. We work closely with our puppy buyers to ensure that each puppy finds its forever home and receives the love and care it deserves.

Our Philosophy

Kennel Shirkus believes in responsible breeding and maintaining the breed standard. We attach great importance to the health, temperament and safety of our dogs. All our breeding animals are carefully selected to meet high standards, and all our puppies are registered and come with a health guarantee.
